Let's make instant noodles!

First, go buy a cup or more (if you'd like) of instant noodles. You can buy any size, any flavour and any brand. You open the cup from the top and you will see small seasoning packets, take them out. Now, for the important part, you have to prepare boiling hot water, approximately 2 and a half cups and dump it into the cup.

Once that is done, you open up the seasoning packets and pour them in the cup. After that, everything would have to be mixed together and you are done! Enjoy your instant noodles!
